KFM 94.5 is a radio station broadcasting in the Western Cape and selected cities of the Eastern and Northern Capes using the frequency 94.5 FM. It is also possible to listen live to its broadcast via live streaming.

 

The station belongs Primedia and presents an adult contemporary format.

Music is the main focus of its programming, playing hits from the past and from today all day long. Its line-up also offers news and sports reports, competitions and entertainment programmes. Its breakfast show, KFM Mornings, with pranks and loads of good humour is the hit show on this radio. Darren and Zoe Brown are the most popular presenters of its line-up.

KFM is also the radio behind some of the most important music events in the Western Cape. KDay, with a line-up of A-list artists and DJs, stands as the most popular of these.

Listeners can also visit the official webpage of KFM to find the podcasts of its shows and the playlist of the show KFM Top 40.
